Asterisk PBX connector for Odoo platform

The primary module

You must purchase a monthly subscription to use this application. For pricing and other details, visit http://odoopbx.com.

Features

  • Odoo open source, enterprise and odoo.sh are supported.
  • Click to call from Partner form.
  • Partners calls history with consolidation on parent company with grouping by partner employees.
  • Call recording, sharing and discussion.
  • Call recording transcript (using external transcript service - https://iap.odoo.com/iap/in-app-services/781)
  • Automatic opening of partner's form on call.
  • Set phone's caller ID name from Odoo partners database.
  • Normalize your phone numbers database.
  • Get caller's salesperson SIP channel and connect customers directly to their managers.
  • Organize differentiated phone customer service by quering customer tags from Asterisk dialplan (e.g. put VIP customers in the VIP queue or in first place).
  • SIP client agnostic.
  • Partner creation from active call or call history.
  • Advanced call history analytics.
  • Automatic call & recordings archiving.
  • Works with the most Asterisk distributions (only AMI connection used).
  • More features coming...
  • Custom development available for request.

Asterisk / FreePBX support

This module requires a running Astrerisk instance.
Network connection between Asterisk and Odoo instance is required.
Please refer to Asterisk documentaion for Asterisk configuration.

If you are a novice in Asterisk/FreePBX and do not know how to complete Asterisk part of installation you can request paid support to help you setup your PBX. Please submit your requests to mailbox@odoopbx.com.
